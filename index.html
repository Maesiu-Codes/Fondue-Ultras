Why choose Fondue?

You can use a cheese mixture from your favorite dairy! Or if you're uncultured: take the ready mix.

You can use white bread - preferably a baguette - or dark bread. Try a kernel bread if you're really adventurous.

Fondue is best enjoyed together. So get together with your loved ones for a Swiss fondue dinner.

There are no limits to where you can eat a Fondue. All you need is a steady heat source, a caquelon, some forks and you're good to go.
